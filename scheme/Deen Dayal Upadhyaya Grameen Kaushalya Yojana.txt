SCHEME NAME: Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY)

DESCRIPTION:
A skill training and placement program by the Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) aimed at transforming poor rural youth into economically independent individuals. It focuses on sustainable employment by providing high-quality, industry-relevant training and ensuring jobs with regular monthly wages at or above the minimum wage. 
*Latest Statistics:* As of recent government data, DDU-GKY has successfully trained over 16.9 lakh candidates and placed over 10.9 lakh candidates in wage employment, achieving an impressive 65% placement rate across top sectors like Textiles, IT-ITES, Retail, and Hospitality.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- The target group is poor rural youth in the age group of 15 to 35 years.
- Age Relaxation: The upper age limit is relaxed to 45 years for women, Persons with Disabilities (PwDs), Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs), Transgenders, and other Special Groups (e.g., rehabilitated bonded labor, victims of trafficking, manual scavengers, HIV-positive persons).
- Poverty Identification: The poor are identified through the Participatory Identification of Poor (PIP) process or the existing Below Poverty Line (BPL) list.
- Alternative Eligibility (If not on the BPL/PIP list, the applicant must meet ONE of the following):
  - Youth from an MGNREGA worker household with at least 15 days of work in the previous financial year.
  - Youth from a household with an RSBY card (with the youth's details mentioned).
  - Youth from a household issued an Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) or BPL PDS card.
  - Youth from a household where a family member is in a Self-Help Group (SHG) under NRLM.
  - Youth from a household covered under auto-inclusion parameters of SECC 2011.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Proof of Identity and Proof of Age
- BPL Card (if applicable)
- MGNREGA Card (if applicable)
- RSBY Card (if applicable)
- AAY Card (if applicable)
- SHG Identification (if applicable)
- Disability Certificate, issued by a competent authority (if applicable)
- ST/SC Certificate, issued by a competent authority (if applicable)
- Self-Certification for Minority Community status (if applicable)

BENEFITS:
- Free Skill Training: Completely free, industry-relevant skill training in sectors like agriculture, construction, retail, hospitality, IT, etc.
- Placement Assistance: Connects beneficiaries with potential employers, providing resume writing and interview prep. (The scheme mandates training partners to place at least 70% of successful candidates).
- Post-Placement Support: Provides mentorship, counseling, and resources to help candidates adjust to new jobs.
- Career Progression Support: Opportunities for advanced training, networking, and career advancement.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Visit the Official "Kaushal Panjee" Website (kaushalpanjee.nic.in).
2. Navigate to "Candidate Registration" to open the Online Registration Form.
3. Select "Fresh/New Registration" under the Registration Type section.
4. Fill in all mandatory fields with personal, educational, and socio-economic details.
5. Upload the required documents and submit the application.
6. Note down the generated Registration ID for future reference.

ESTIMATED PROCESSING TIME: Registration is instant. Candidates are mobilized and batched for training programs as per local Project Implementing Agency (PIA) schedules.

APPLICATION FEES: Free (The training, uniform, study material, and accommodation/food for residential courses are 100% funded by the government).

CONTACT INFORMATION:
Register online at Kaushal Panjee (kaushalpanjee.nic.in) or contact your local Gram Panchayat, State Rural Livelihood Mission (SRLM) office, or nearby DDU-GKY training center.
