SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)

DESCRIPTION:
A flagship scheme by the Government of India facilitating collateral-free micro-credit/loans up to ₹20 lakhs to income-generating micro-enterprises. It targets the non-corporate, non-farm sector in manufacturing, trading, or service sectors, including allied agricultural activities (poultry, dairy, beekeeping, etc.). Loans are extended directly by Member Lending Institutions like Public/Private Banks, Micro Finance Institutions (MFIs), and NBFCs. 

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Eligible entities include Individuals, Proprietary concerns, Partnership Firms, Private Ltd. Companies, Public Companies, and other legal forms.
- Must be running or starting an income-generating micro-enterprise in the non-farm manufacturing, trading, or service sectors (or allied agriculture).
- The applicant must NOT be a defaulter to any bank or financial institution and must have a satisfactory credit track record.
- The borrower may be required to possess the necessary skills, experience, or educational knowledge to undertake the proposed business activity.
- *Tarun Plus Exception*: Only entrepreneurs who have already availed and successfully repaid previous loans under the 'Tarun' category are eligible for the newly introduced 'Tarun Plus' category (₹10 lakh to ₹20 lakh).

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
*For Shishu Loan (Up to ₹50,000):*
- Proof of Identity (Self-attested Voter ID, Driving License, PAN Card, Aadhaar, or Passport).
- Proof of Residence (Recent utility bill < 2 months, Voter ID, Aadhaar, Passport, or Domicile Certificate).
- Applicant’s recent colored Photograph (2 copies, not older than 6 months).
- Quotation of Machinery/items to be purchased (Supplier name, machinery details, and price).
- Proof of Identity/Address of the Business Enterprise (Relevant licenses or registration certificates, if applicable).

*For Kishore, Tarun, and Tarun Plus Loans (Above ₹50,000 to ₹20 Lakhs):*
- All basic KYC documents listed above (ID Proof, Residence Proof, 2 recent Photographs, Business Identity/Address Proof).
- Statement of accounts from the existing banker for the last 6 months.
- Last 2 years' balance sheets of the unit along with income/sales tax returns (Mandatory for loans of ₹2 Lakhs and above).
- Projected balance sheets for 1 year (working capital) or the loan period (term loan) (Mandatory for loans of ₹2 Lakhs and above).
- Sales achieved during the current financial year up to the date of submission.
- Project Report for the proposed project containing technical & economic viability details.
- Memorandum and Articles of Association (MoA/AoA) or Partnership Deed, if applicable.
- Asset & Liability statement from the borrower (including Directors & Partners) in the absence of a third-party guarantee.

BENEFITS:
- Collateral-Free Loans classified into four growth-stage categories:
  - Shishu: Loans up to ₹50,000.
  - Kishore: Loans above ₹50,000 and up to ₹5 Lakhs.
  - Tarun: Loans above ₹5 Lakhs and up to ₹10 Lakhs.
  - Tarun Plus: Loans above ₹10 Lakhs and up to ₹20 Lakhs.
- Subsidized Processing: Upfront fee/processing charges for Shishu loans are completely waived by most banks. 

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Visit the official PM MUDRA website and navigate to the Udyamimitra portal or Jan Samarth portal.
2. Click on "Apply Now" for a Mudra loan.
3. Select your entrepreneur type (New, Existing, or Self-employed professional).
4. Register by filling in the applicant's name, email, and mobile number, and generate an OTP.
5. After successful registration, fill in your Personal Details and Professional Details.
6. Select a hand-holding agency if you need help preparing project proposals, otherwise click "Loan Application Center".
7. Select the required loan category (Shishu, Kishore, Tarun, or Tarun Plus) and enter your business/industry information.
8. Attach all required documents (KYC, Business proof, Financial statements).
9. Submit the application to generate an Application Number for future reference and bank follow-up.

ESTIMATED PROCESSING TIME: Subject to bank verification of business viability, credit history, and document completeness (typically 1 to 3 weeks).

APPLICATION FEES: Application is free. (Processing fees are waived for Shishu; nominal processing charges may apply for higher categories as per individual bank guidelines).

CONTACT INFORMATION:
Apply online via Udyamimitra (udyamimitra.in) or Jan Samarth (jansamarth.in), or visit any local Public/Private Sector Bank, Regional Rural Bank, or NBFC offline.
