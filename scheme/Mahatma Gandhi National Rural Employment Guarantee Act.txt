SCHEME NAME: Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

DESCRIPTION:
A flagship rural livelihood and employment guarantee scheme by the Ministry of Rural Development. It guarantees at least 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. It aims to enhance livelihood security, reduce rural migration, and create durable assets (like water conservation structures and rural roads).

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Must be a citizen of India residing in a rural area.
- The applicant must be at least 18 years of age at the time of application.
- The applicant must volunteer to do unskilled manual work.
- The scheme is open to all rural households, irrespective of their income or poverty line (BPL/APL) status.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Aadhaar Card (Mandatory for Aadhaar Based Payment System / wage transfer).
- Passport-sized photograph.
- Identity and Age Proof (Voter ID, Aadhaar, or PAN Card).
- Bank Account or Post Office Account details.
- Ration Card or local address proof.

BENEFITS:
- Guaranteed Employment: 100 days of guaranteed wage employment per household per financial year.
- Timely Wages: Wages are strictly paid within 15 days of work completion directly into the bank account (DBT).
- Unemployment Allowance: If work is not provided within 15 days of demanding it, the state must pay an unemployment allowance to the applicant.
- Worksite Facilities: Includes safe drinking water, shade for resting, a first-aid box, and a creche for children under 6 years old if more than five children are present.
- Close Proximity: Work is generally provided within a 5 km radius of the village. If it is further away, extra wages (10%) are paid for transportation.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Visit the local Gram Panchayat office.
2. Submit a verbal or written application for registration, providing names, age, and address details of adult members willing to work.
3. The Gram Panchayat verifies the local residence of the household.
4. Upon verification, the Gram Panchayat issues a free "Job Card" containing the photographs of the registered adult members.
5. Once you have the Job Card, submit a formal application for work (specifying the dates and duration you want to work) to the Gram Panchayat.
6. The Panchayat allocates work and issues a dated receipt of your work application.

ESTIMATED PROCESSING TIME: The Gram Panchayat must issue the Job Card within 15 days of registration. Once work is requested, it must be allocated within 15 days.

APPLICATION FEES: Free (Registration and Job Card issuance are entirely free of cost).

CONTACT INFORMATION:
Apply offline directly at your local Gram Panchayat or Block Development Office (BDO). Website: nrega.nic.in
