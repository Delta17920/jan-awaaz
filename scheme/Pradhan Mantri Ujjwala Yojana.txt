SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana 2.0 (PMUY)

DESCRIPTION:
A flagship scheme by the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MOPNG) to provide free LPG (liquefied petroleum gas) connections to women from below-poverty-line and deprived households. It aims to replace traditional, health-damaging cooking fuels (firewood, coal, cow-dung cakes) with clean cooking fuel. Ujjwala 2.0 includes a special facility providing deposit-free connections for migrant households.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- The applicant must be an adult woman (attained 18 years of age).
- There must NOT be any other active LPG connection in the same household.
- The applicant must belong to one of the following categories: SC/ST Households, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin) beneficiaries, Most Backward Classes, Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) beneficiaries, Tea and Ex-Tea Garden tribes, Forest Dwellers, People residing on Islands/River Islands, SECC Households (AHL TIN), or any Poor Household under the 14-point declaration.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Duly filled Know Your Customer (KYC) Form.
- Ration Card issued by the State (or other State Govt. document certifying family composition).
- *MIGRANT EXCEPTION*: Migrants can submit a Self-Declaration for Family Composition and Address (Annexure I) if they do not have a local Ration Card.
- Aadhaar Card of the beneficiary and all adult family members.
- Proof of Address (Aadhaar acts as Proof of Identity and Address if the connection is at the same address).
- Bank Account Number and IFSC Code.

BENEFITS:
- Free LPG Connection: Cash assistance of Rs. 1600 (for a 14.2kg cylinder) or Rs. 1150 (for a 5kg cylinder) provided by the Government.
- Cost Breakdown Covered: Includes Security Deposit for the cylinder (Rs. 1250/800), Pressure Regulator (Rs. 150), LPG Hose (Rs. 100), Domestic Gas Consumer Card (Rs. 25), and Inspection/Installation charges (Rs. 75).
- Ujjwala 2.0 Special Benefit: The first LPG refill and a hotplate (Stove) are provided absolutely FREE of cost by the Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) along with the deposit-free connection.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Obtain the forms (KYC Form, Supplementary KYC Document) online or from your nearest LPG distributor.
2. Fill out the application form with personal details, bank account information, and LPG distributor preference.
3. Attach copies of the necessary documents (Aadhaar, Ration Card/Migrant Declaration, Bank details).
4. Submit the completed application form and supporting documents directly to the nearest LPG distributor.
5. A mechanic will conduct a Pre-Installation check of the premises (Annexure II) before releasing the connection.

ESTIMATED PROCESSING TIME: Connections are usually released within a few days after document verification and the pre-installation premises check.

APPLICATION FEES: Free (The connection, first refill, and stove are 100% free for the beneficiary).

CONTACT INFORMATION:
Submit applications offline directly to any nearest local LPG distributor (Indane, Bharat Gas, or HP Gas).
