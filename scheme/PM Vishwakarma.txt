SCHEME NAME: PM Vishwakarma

DESCRIPTION:
A Central Sector Scheme launched by the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MoMSME) to provide end-to-end support to artisans and craftspeople. It offers collateral-free credit, skill training, modern tools, digital transaction incentives, and market linkage. 
*Covered Trades:* The scheme specifically covers 18 family-based traditional trades: Carpenter (Suthar), Boat Maker, Armourer, Blacksmith (Lohar), Hammer and Tool Kit Maker, Locksmith, Goldsmith (Sunar), Potter (Kumhaar), Sculptor/Stone Carver, Cobbler/Shoemaker, Mason (Rajmistri), Basket/Mat/Broom Maker, Doll & Toy Maker, Barber (Naai), Garland Maker (Malakaar), Washerman (Dhobi), Tailor (Darzi), and Fishing Net Maker.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Must be an artisan or craftsperson working with hands and tools in one of the 18 recognized traditional trades.
- Must be engaged in the unorganized sector on a self-employment basis.
- Minimum age of the applicant must be 18 years on the date of registration.
- Must be currently engaged in the concerned trade on the date of registration.
- Must NOT have availed loans under similar credit-based self-employment schemes (e.g., PMEGP, PM SVANidhi, Mudra) in the past 5 years. (Exception: MUDRA/SVANidhi beneficiaries who have fully repaid their loans are eligible).
- EXCLUSIONS: Registration is restricted to only ONE member per family. Persons in government service and their family members are NOT eligible.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Aadhaar Card (Mandatory for e-KYC and registration).
- Mobile Number (must be linked to Aadhaar).
- Bank Account Details.
- Ration Card (Mandatory. If the beneficiary does not have a ration card, they must produce the Aadhaar cards of all family members).

BENEFITS:
- Recognition: Official PM Vishwakarma Certificate and Digital ID Card.
- Skilling & Stipend: Basic Training (5-7 days / 40 hours) and Advanced Training (15 days / 120 hours). Artisans receive a training stipend of ₹500 per day.
- Toolkit Incentive: A grant of ₹15,000 to purchase modern tools and equipment.
- Collateral-Free Credit: Enterprise Development Loans provided in two tranches. ₹1,00,000 (First Tranche, 18-month repayment) and ₹2,00,000 (Second Tranche, 30-month repayment).
- Subsidized Interest: Beneficiaries pay a highly concessional interest rate of just 5% (the government covers an interest subvention cap up to 8% and credit guarantee fees).
- Digital Transactions: Incentive of ₹1 per digital transaction (maximum up to 100 transactions monthly).
- Marketing Support: Assistance with quality certification, branding, e-commerce linkage, and trade fair participation.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or the official PM Vishwakarma portal.
2. Complete Aadhaar Verification by entering the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.
3. Complete the Biometric Verification Process at the CSC.
4. Fill in the mandatory details in the Online Application Form and submit it to generate an Application Number.
5. The application undergoes a 3-stage verification process: Gram Panchayat/ULB level, District Implementation Committee, and finally the Screening Committee.
6. Upon final approval, the artisan receives their Vishwakarma ID and can begin training/availing funds.

ESTIMATED PROCESSING TIME: Varies based on the 3-stage local and district verification process. Notifications regarding the disbursal of incentives are sent via SMS.

APPLICATION FEES: Free registration. 

CONTACT INFORMATION:
Apply online via the PM Vishwakarma Portal (pmvishwakarma.gov.in) or visit your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) / Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE) for assisted application.
