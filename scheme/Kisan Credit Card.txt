SCHEME NAME: Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

DESCRIPTION:
The KCC Scheme provides adequate and timely credit support from the banking system under a single window to farmers for their cultivation and other needs. This includes short-term credit for crop cultivation, post-harvest expenses, produce marketing, consumption requirements, working capital for farm maintenance, and term loans for agriculture and allied activities. It is delivered via ATM-enabled RuPay cards, smart cards, or Aadhaar-enabled biometric cards.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Individual or joint borrowers who are owner cultivators.
- Tenant farmers, oral lessees, and sharecroppers.
- Self Help Groups (SHGs) or Joint Liability Groups (JLGs) of farmers, including tenant farmers and sharecroppers.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Duly filled Application Form
- Two Passport Size Photographs
- ID Proof (Aadhaar Card, Driving License, Voter ID, or Passport)
- Address Proof (Aadhaar Card or Driving License)
- Proof of Landholding (duly certified by revenue authorities)
- Cropping pattern (Crops grown) with acreage details
- Security/Collateral documents (required ONLY for loan limits above Rs. 1.60 lakhs or Rs. 3.00 lakhs, depending on bank policy)

BENEFITS:
- Subsidized Interest Rates: The government provides a 2% interest subvention and a 3% Prompt Repayment Incentive, making the effective credit rate highly subsidized at 4% per annum.
- Flexible Credit Limits: Fixed for 5 years. The first year is based on the scale of finance, cultivated area, plus 10% for post-harvest/consumption and 20% for maintenance/insurance.
- Limit Escalation: The credit limit automatically increases by 10% every successive year (years 2 through 5) to account for cost escalation.
- Term Loans: Available for long-term investments like land development, minor irrigation, and purchasing farm equipment.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Visit the official website of your preferred participating bank or visit the bank branch offline.
2. Select the 'Kisan Credit Card' scheme option and click 'Apply'.
3. Fill in the required details in the application form.
4. Upload or submit copies of your KYC, landholding proof, and cropping details.
5. Click 'Submit' to receive an application reference number.
6. The bank will review your eligibility and contact you for further processing.

ESTIMATED PROCESSING TIME: The bank typically responds within 3-4 working days after application submission.

APPLICATION FEES: Standard banking processing fees may apply depending on the bank.

CONTACT INFORMATION:
Available through all participating Public Sector Banks, Private Banks, Cooperative Banks, and Regional Rural Banks (RRBs).
