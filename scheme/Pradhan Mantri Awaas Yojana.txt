SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G)

DESCRIPTION:
The flagship rural housing mission by the Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) aimed at providing a pucca house with basic amenities to all houseless households and those living in kutcha and dilapidated houses. The scheme was recently extended by the Union Cabinet for another 5 years (FY 2024–25 to 2028–29) to construct additional rural houses. 
*Up-to-Date Statistics (As of August 2025):* The cumulative target now stands at 4.95 crore houses. A total of 3.85 crore houses have been sanctioned, and over 2.82 crore houses have been completed. 

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Must be a houseless household or living in a zero, one, or two-room house with kutcha walls and kutcha roofs (identified via SECC data and verified by the Gram Sabha).
- Automatic/Compulsory Inclusion: Households without shelter, destitutes/living on alms, manual scavengers, Primitive Tribal Groups (PTGs), and legally released bonded laborers.
- Prioritization Score: Preference is given based on socio-economic deprivation (e.g., no adult member 16-59, female-headed household with no adult male, no literate adult above 25, disabled member with no able-bodied adult, landless casual labor).
- Target Demographics: 60% of the target allocated to each State/UT is earmarked for Scheduled Castes (SC) and Scheduled Tribes (ST). 15% of the total fund at the National Level is earmarked for Minorities. 3% of beneficiaries at the State Level are prioritized for Persons with Disabilities (PwDs).
- EXCLUSIONS: You are NOT eligible if you live in a pucca house, have >2 rooms, own a motorized 2/3/4-wheeler or fishing boat, own mechanized agricultural equipment, have a Kisan Credit Card limit ≥ ₹50,000, are a government employee, earn > ₹10,000/month, pay income/professional tax, own a refrigerator or landline, or own 2.5+ acres of irrigated land.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Aadhaar Card (self-attested copy). If illiterate, a consent letter with a thumbprint is required.
- MGNREGA Job Card (duly registered)
- Bank Account Details (Original and duplicate)
- Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM) Number
- Affidavit stating the beneficiary or family members do not own a pucca house.

BENEFITS:
- Financial Assistance: ₹1,20,000 per unit in plain areas; ₹1,30,000 per unit in hilly areas, difficult areas, and IAP districts (Himalayan states, NE states, J&K, Ladakh).
- Housing Specifications: Minimum size of 25 sq. m, including a dedicated area for hygienic cooking.
- Institutional Loan Option: Beneficiaries can avail a loan up to ₹70,000 at a 3% lower interest rate (maximum principal for subsidy is ₹2,00,000).
- Convergence Benefits: 
  - ₹12,000 for toilet construction under Swachh Bharat Mission-Gramin (SBM-G).
  - 90/95 days of unskilled labor wage employment under MGNREGA.
  - 1 Free LPG connection under PM Ujjwala Yojana, plus piped water and electricity connections.
- 100% Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) electronically to the Aadhaar-linked bank/post-office account.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Visit the official PMAY-G web portal or the Awaas+ App.
2. Enter personal details (Gender, Mobile number, Aadhaar number) and upload the Aadhaar consent form.
3. Search for the beneficiary name using the PMAY ID or priority list.
4. Click 'Select to Register' to auto-generate and fill in remaining details (ownership type, relation, etc.).
5. Enter Bank account information and select 'Yes' if availing a loan.
6. Provide convergence details (MGNREGA job card number and SBM number).
7. Submit the application to the concerned office for final verification and sanction.

ESTIMATED PROCESSING TIME: Verification is conducted by the Gram Sabha before the sanction letter is issued. Construction funds are released in 3 installments based on construction progress (Plinth, Roof, Completion).

APPLICATION FEES: Free.

CONTACT INFORMATION:
Apply online via the PMAY-G portal (pmayg.nic.in), the Awaas+ mobile app, or contact your local Gram Panchayat / Block Development Office.
